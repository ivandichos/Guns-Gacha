About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play, open-world action RPG developed by miHoYo. Explore the vast world of Teyvat, uncovering its mysteries and engaging in thrilling combat.

The game features a diverse cast of characters, each with unique abilities and playstyles. Collect and upgrade your characters to build your ultimate team and conquer challenging bosses and domains.

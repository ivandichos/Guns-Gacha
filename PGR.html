About Punishing Gray Raven

Punishing: Gray Raven is a fast-paced, stylish free-to-play action RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a biomechanical virus called the Punishing.

Players take on the role of the Commandant, leading a squad of powerful cyborg soldiers known as Constructs to fight back against the Corrupted and reclaim their planet.

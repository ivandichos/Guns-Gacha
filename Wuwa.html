About Wuthering Waves

Wuthering Waves is a free-to-play, story-rich open-world action RPG set in a post-apocalyptic world called Solaris-3.

Players take on the role of the Rover, an amnesiac who awakens to a world desolated by a catastrophe known as the Lament, which has brought forth dangerous beings called Tacet Discords.
